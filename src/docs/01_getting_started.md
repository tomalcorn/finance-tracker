# Getting started

This app helps you keep your personal finances in one place:

- Budget Tracker rows show how money is allocated
- Expense and Income Sources roll up into those Budget Trackers
- Payments record the actual money moving in and out
- Bank Accounts help cross check all your payments are up to date
- One-Offs track savings goals that build up over time
- Subscriptions list recurring payments and feed into Payments

## Authentication

Login is handled through [Auth0](https://auth0.com).

## Initial Setup

The usual setup order is:

1. Add your bank accounts.
2. Create expense sources to match how you want to categorise spending.
3. Create income sources and link them to the right budget tracker rows.
4. Start entering payments and subscriptions.
5. Use the one-offs block for savings goals like holidays, gifts, large purchases, or
   anything that isn't regular monthly spending.

## A Good First Check

If you are new to the app, start by checking these three things:

- do your bank accounts exist with the right starting balances?
- do the budget tracker categories match how you want to think about money?
- are your main expense and income sources linked to the right tracker rows?
